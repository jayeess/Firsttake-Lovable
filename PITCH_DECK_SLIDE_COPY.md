# Pitch Deck Slide Copy

Ready-to-copy text for a 10-slide FirstTake / Nata Connect pitch deck.

## Slide 1: FirstTake by MVA Studios

Subtitle:

Where Talent Meets Opportunity.

Bullets:

- Nata Connect is a casting workflow for Talent and verified recruiters.
- Built for profile, audition, application, review, and trust operations.
- Starting Telugu/Tollywood-first with a controlled beta approach.

Footer note:

MVP ready for founder-led beta demos.

## Slide 2: The Problem

Subtitle:

Casting is still too fragmented.

Bullets:

- Auditions are scattered across social posts, chats, forms, and referrals.
- Talent often do not know what is real or where their application stands.
- Recruiters review applicants across disconnected messages and spreadsheets.
- Trust and safety risks are harder to spot without a clear record.

Footer note:

The pain is workflow fragmentation, not lack of Talent.

## Slide 3: Target Users

Subtitle:

Focused first on emerging Talent and serious casting teams.

Bullets:

- Talent: actors, models, dancers, anchors, and voice artists.
- Recruiters: casting directors, producers, agencies, studios, and film teams.
- Admins: founder/operators responsible for verification and safety.
- Initial wedge: Telugu/Tollywood-first creative community.

Footer note:

Start focused, learn responsibly, expand after proof.

## Slide 4: The Solution

Subtitle:

One connected casting loop.

Bullets:

- Talent build credible profiles and portfolio signals.
- Recruiters publish structured casting briefs.
- Applications move through a visible status pipeline.
- Messages, notifications, self-tape links, and decisions stay connected.
- Admins manage verification, reports, moderation, and launch readiness.

Footer note:

Nata Connect organizes casting without automating casting judgment.

## Slide 5: Product Walkthrough

Subtitle:

The MVP supports the core marketplace workflow.

Bullets:

- Talent profile completeness, media, links, and trust indicators.
- Audition discovery with search, filters, saved roles, and details.
- Application tracker from applied to final decision.
- Recruiter review room with applicant profiles, notes, stages, and messages.

Footer note:

Self-tapes are external links only in this MVP.

## Slide 6: Trust and Safety

Subtitle:

Trust is built into the workflow.

Bullets:

- Recruiter verification with admin review.
- Optional Talent verification and visible trust badges.
- Reports, moderation actions, and audit logs.
- Application-linked messaging instead of open random inboxes.
- Clear "never pay to audition" safety guidance.

Footer note:

Human review remains part of the early trust model.

## Slide 7: Why Telugu / Tollywood First

Subtitle:

A focused market wedge for a trust-sensitive workflow.

Bullets:

- Active creative ecosystem with demand for new Talent.
- Mobile-first discovery behavior and relationship-driven casting.
- Regional identity helps the product feel specific, not generic.
- Focused launch makes feedback and trust operations manageable.

Footer note:

Regional-first is a strategy, not a limitation.

## Slide 8: MVP Progress

Subtitle:

Working product, tested core flows.

Bullets:

- Public, Talent, Recruiter, and Admin routes are implemented.
- Firebase Auth, Firestore, Storage, Admin SDK, and rules are in place.
- Application, messaging, notification, verification, and report flows exist.
- Live production smoke test found zero launch-blocker bugs.
- Lint, tests, and production build are clean.

Footer note:

Current status: ready for controlled beta, not broad public scale.

## Slide 9: Beta Launch Plan

Subtitle:

Small, founder-led, measured.

Bullets:

- Invite 10-20 early Talent and 2-5 trusted recruiters.
- Monitor profile completion, auditions posted, applications, and response time.
- Review verification, reports, and safety signals daily.
- Collect feedback before expanding the cohort.
- Improve the workflow based on real usage, not assumptions.

Footer note:

The beta goal is learning, trust, and activation.

## Slide 10: Ask / Next Step

Subtitle:

Help validate the first controlled casting network.

Bullets:

- Talent: join the beta and build a real profile.
- Recruiters: verify and post one clear casting brief.
- Mentors: review the workflow and operating plan.
- Incubators/investors: support responsible beta validation.
- FirstTake: measure the loop before scaling.

Footer note:

Next milestone: controlled beta with real users and daily trust operations.
