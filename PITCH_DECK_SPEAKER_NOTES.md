# Pitch Deck Speaker Notes

Use these notes with `PITCH_DECK_OUTLINE.md` and
`PITCH_DECK_SLIDE_COPY.md`. The same 10-slide deck can be delivered as a
3-minute pitch, 5-minute pitch, or 8-minute demo pitch.

## Global Speaking Rules

Emphasize:

- Casting workflow clarity
- Trust-led marketplace design
- Telugu/Tollywood-first launch focus
- Controlled beta as the next milestone
- Real MVP progress without inflated claims

Do not overclaim:

- No guaranteed jobs or guaranteed casting
- No fake traction or user counts
- No fake partnerships
- No enterprise-scale production readiness
- No AI, payment, calendar, video-call, or direct video-upload claims
- Self-tapes are external links only

Strong closing line:

"We are not trying to make casting automatic. We are making casting more
organized, safer, and easier to trust."

## 3-Minute Pitch

Target pace:

About 15 to 20 seconds per slide. Keep the product walkthrough high-level and
skip detailed route-by-route explanation.

### Opening Line

"FirstTake by MVA Studios is building Nata Connect, a trusted casting workflow
for Talent and verified recruiters. Our promise is simple: Where Talent Meets
Opportunity."

### Slide 1: Title

Say:

"This is a regional-first casting platform built for the real journey from
profile to audition to application to recruiter decision."

Transition:

"The reason this matters is the current casting workflow is still fragmented."

### Slide 2: Problem

Say:

"Talent discover opportunities through social media, chats, forms, and
referrals. Recruiters collect applicants through scattered links and messages.
Both sides lose context, and trust issues become harder to detect."

Transition:

"So we are focused on the users who feel this pain first."

### Slide 3: Target Users

Say:

"The first users are emerging performers and serious casting teams. We are
starting Telugu/Tollywood-first because it is culturally specific, mobile-first,
and trust-sensitive."

Transition:

"The solution is one connected casting loop."

### Slide 4: Solution

Say:

"Nata Connect connects profile, audition, application, recruiter review,
messages, notifications, and admin trust operations in one workflow."

Transition:

"The product already supports that core loop."

### Slide 5: Product Walkthrough

Say:

"Talent can complete profiles, browse auditions, save roles, apply, and track
status. Recruiters can verify, post casting briefs, and review applicants.
Admins can manage verification, reports, moderation, and launch readiness."

Transition:

"In casting, trust is not optional."

### Slide 6: Trust and Safety

Say:

"The MVP includes recruiter verification, optional Talent verification, reports,
audit logs, admin moderation, and application-linked messaging. It also repeats
the core safety rule: never pay to audition."

Transition:

"The launch focus is intentionally regional."

### Slide 7: Why Telugu / Tollywood First

Say:

"A Telugu/Tollywood-first launch gives us a focused creative market, a clearer
identity, and a manageable first beta group before expansion."

Transition:

"Here is where the product stands today."

### Slide 8: MVP Progress

Say:

"This is a working MVP, not just a mockup. The core routes, Firebase Auth,
Firestore, Storage, Admin SDK flows, verification, reports, messaging, and
notifications are in place. The latest smoke test found no launch-blocker bugs."

Transition:

"The next step is controlled beta, not broad launch."

### Slide 9: Beta Launch Plan

Say:

"We want to invite a small cohort: 10 to 20 Talent and 2 to 5 trusted
recruiters. We will measure profile completion, auditions posted, applications,
response time, reports, and trust issues."

Transition:

"The ask is simple."

### Slide 10: Ask / Next Step

Say:

"Help us validate the first controlled casting network: introduce trusted
Talent, introduce legitimate recruiters, or support the beta review process."

Close:

"FirstTake is not promising instant casting. It is building a safer workflow so
serious Talent and serious casting teams can work with more clarity."

## 5-Minute Pitch

Target pace:

About 25 to 35 seconds per slide. Add one concrete product example on slides 5
and 6.

### Opening Line

"Casting is a high-trust process, but the digital workflow around it is still
fragmented. FirstTake is building Nata Connect to make that workflow clearer
for Talent, recruiters, and the people operating the platform."

### Slide-by-Slide Notes

Slide 1:

"Lead with the brand and product promise. Keep it simple. FirstTake is the
company/product initiative by MVA Studios; Nata Connect is the platform."

Slide 2:

"Use one example: a role may be posted on Instagram, discussed on WhatsApp,
collected through a form, and tracked in a spreadsheet. Nobody has one clean
record."

Slide 3:

"Mention that early focus matters. We are not trying to onboard every creative
category at once. We are starting where the product can be tested responsibly."

Slide 4:

"Describe the loop, not just features. The loop is the product: profile,
audition, application, review, decision, communication, trust."

Slide 5:

"Give the walkthrough in product terms. Talent see profile completeness and
auditions. Recruiters see structured briefs and applicants. Admins see queues
and readiness. This makes the MVP feel real without live-clicking every route."

Slide 6:

"Be firm that trust is operational. Verification, reports, audit logs, and
moderation are not later slides; they are part of the MVP because casting has
real safety concerns."

Slide 7:

"Regional-first is the wedge. It helps messaging, onboarding, cultural fit, and
early feedback. It also prevents the product from feeling like a generic global
job board."

Slide 8:

"Do not turn progress into traction. Say what is built and tested. Do not imply
large usage. The strongest proof today is working product depth and readiness
discipline."

Slide 9:

"The beta plan should sound operational. Daily admin review, limited recruiter
onboarding, clear safety checks, and honest feedback loops."

Slide 10:

"Make the ask match the room. For Talent, join. For recruiters, verify and post
one brief. For mentors, critique the flow. For investors/incubators, support
responsible beta validation."

### 5-Minute Closing Line

"We have a focused MVP, a clear trust model, and a practical beta plan. The next
step is not hype. It is real usage with a small group and disciplined learning."

## 8-Minute Demo Pitch

Target pace:

About 40 to 50 seconds per slide. Pair the slide deck with live route demos or
screenshots from `LIVE_DEMO_ROUTE_ORDER.md`.

### Opening Line

"I will show FirstTake as both a product story and a working workflow. The
important thing to watch is how every screen connects back to the same casting
loop: profile, audition, application, review, decision, and trust."

### Slide 1 to 2 Transition

"Before showing screens, I want to name the problem clearly."

What to emphasize:

- Fragmented discovery
- Unclear application status
- Recruiter review chaos
- Trust and safety risk

What not to overclaim:

- Do not say all casting is broken.
- Do not criticize recruiters as the problem.

### Slide 3 to 4 Transition

"Because this is a trust-sensitive workflow, we are starting with a focused set
of users and a focused product loop."

What to emphasize:

- Emerging Talent
- Serious recruiters
- Founder/admin operations
- Regional-first wedge

### Slide 5 Demo Moment

Suggested live route sequence:

1. `/`
2. `/dashboard`
3. `/talent/profile`
4. `/auditions`
5. `/applications`
6. `/recruiter/auditions/[id]/applicants`

Say:

"Notice the product is not just a list of auditions. It is an operating record.
Talent prepare their profile, apply with context, and track status. Recruiters
review in stages and keep notes and messages attached to the audition."

What to emphasize:

- Profile completeness
- Search and saved auditions
- Application pack
- Status pipeline
- Applicant review room

What not to overclaim:

- Do not say self-tape video upload exists.
- Do not say recruiter decisions are automated.

### Slide 6 Demo Moment

Suggested live routes:

1. `/recruiter/verification`
2. `/admin`
3. `/admin/beta-readiness`
4. `/admin/reports` if safe data exists

Say:

"This is the part that makes it more than a listing site. The platform has
verification, reports, audit logs, and launch readiness because early trust
operations have to be visible."

What to emphasize:

- Manual verification
- Admin review
- Reports
- Auditability
- Safe copy

What not to overclaim:

- Do not say the system removes all fraud.
- Do not imply reports are automatically resolved.

### Slide 7 Regional Focus

Say:

"The Telugu/Tollywood-first focus gives the product a community and identity.
It also lets us test the workflow with a reachable group instead of pretending
to serve every market on day one."

### Slide 8 MVP Progress

Say:

"The most important progress signal is that the product loop exists and has
been checked end to end. The live production smoke test inspected public,
Talent, Recruiter, Admin, API, Firestore, and Storage flows and found no
launch-blocker bugs."

What not to overclaim:

- Do not say this means enterprise readiness.
- Do not replace beta validation with test results.

### Slide 9 Beta Plan

Say:

"A controlled beta is the right next step: small cohort, daily admin review,
clear safety monitoring, and feedback before expansion."

What to emphasize:

- 10-20 Talent
- 2-5 trusted recruiters
- Daily report/verification review
- Measure activation and trust

### Slide 10 Ask

Say:

"If you are Talent, help us test by building a real profile. If you are a
casting team, verify and post one serious brief. If you are a mentor or
incubator, help us pressure-test the workflow and operating model."

Strong closing line:

"FirstTake is building the trustworthy workflow layer for casting. We are ready
for controlled beta learning, and we want the right early users to help shape
it responsibly."

## Backup Lines for Common Questions

If asked about traction:

"The current claim is product readiness for controlled beta, not user traction.
The next milestone is measured beta usage."

If asked about monetization:

"Payments and subscriptions are not part of this MVP. We need to validate the
trusted casting loop first."

If asked about AI:

"AI is not in this version. The current priority is workflow clarity, trust, and
human review."

If asked about video:

"Self-tapes use external links only right now. Direct video upload can be
considered later after storage, moderation, and cost requirements are proven."

If asked about safety:

"Safety is handled through verification, reports, audit logs, admin moderation,
application-linked messaging, and clear no-payment guidance. It reduces risk
and creates a review trail, but it still requires active operations."
